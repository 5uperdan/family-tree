Welcome to the homepage of the Barthaud family tree. Free name data is limited on the internet, but it seems as though there are fewer than 200 people with the surname. The aim of this site is to map, as best as possible, the relationship between people who share the surname or those with close ties to it. It is intended to be inclusive, and if you feel like you should be on the tree then you can be added. This isn't meant to serve as an exhaustive list, but here are some examples of individuals I'm happy to include:
  • Any adopted or fostered person.
  • Anyone who has married into the family or who parents a child of the family.
  • Any child of a parent who would belong on this tree.
If you would like to be included, have information about individuals who should be added, or would like your information removed, please get in touch tree@barthaud.com.
